Clean the existing distribute folder:
	rm -r OpenREM.egg-info
	rm -r openrem/remapp/migrations
Or just remove all the contents!

Then get the new contents:
	rsync -av ../bbOpenREM/openrem .
	rsync -av ../bbOpenREM/distribute/ .

Tidy up:
	find . -name *.pyc -exec rm {} \;
	rm -r openrem/remapp/migrations
	mv openrem/openrem/settings.py{,.example}
	mv openrem/openrem/wsgi.py{,.example}

Build:
	python setup.py sdist
